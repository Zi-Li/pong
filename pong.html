Pong

Instructions:
Press W to move up, and S to move down.
Hit the edges of the paddle to send the ball flying at high speeds.
Gain rage by scoring points. Use rage to fire off special abilities.
Press E to grow the paddle (cost 1 rage, lasts until next point).
Press R to teleport to the ball's yPosition (cost 1 rage, instantaneous use).
Hold down R to continue sliding in that direction.
Press Q to shrink the opponent's paddle (cost 1 rage, lasts until next point).
The first to seven points wins.
After the game ends, press spacebar to restart.

Control Summary:
W: move up
S: move down
E: paddle growth (cost 1 rage)
R: teleport (cost 1 rage)
Q: shrink ray (cost 1 rage)
Spacebar: restart after game ends
